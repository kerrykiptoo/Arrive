Arriv√© provides direct booking infrastructure for short stay properties in Kenya. When you interact with our system ‚Äî through Instagram DM or a property using our platform ‚Äî some of your personal information is processed. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

Arriv√© operates as a data processor. The property you are booking with is the data controller. They are responsible for decisions about how your data is used. We process it on their behalf under their instruction.

What We Collect
  • Full name ‚Äî provided during booking
  • Email address ‚Äî for confirmation and follow-up communications
  • Phone number ‚Äî for booking records and M-Pesa payment
  • Booking details ‚Äî unit selected, check-in and check-out dates, amount paid

  • The property you are booking ‚Äî they receive your name, email, phone, and booking details as the data controller

  • SendGrid (Twilio) ‚Äî for email delivery only
