Questions? We've got answers

Everything you need to know about hosting with Arrive.

How do I get paid? +
Guests pay via M-Pesa or card. Funds are sent directly to your account. No holding, no delays, no commission taken.
What if I need to cancel a booking? +
You can cancel from your dashboard. We recommend setting clear cancellation policies upfront. Guests are notified immediately and refunded per your policy.
Do I need to be tech-savvy? +
Not at all. Everything happens in chat and your dashboard. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can host on Arrive.
How do guests find my listing? +
You share your unique link — arrive.co.ke/yourname. Post it anywhere: Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp status, or word of mouth. Guests book directly.
What if I want to stop hosting? +
Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No contracts. No exit fees. Your listing is removed, and you keep all your earnings.
